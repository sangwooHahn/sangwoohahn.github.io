Museum Branding

MoMap

The MoMap branding project designs logos and color systems for use in map museums. Explain how to use the branding system and introduce applications such as posters and web designs that can be used as examples.

Conception

MoMap is a museum that collects maps of the world. There are many maps available from the past to the present. There is also an experience hall where you can make maps with measurements and geographic information.

Introduction

MoMap is a museum where people can see various maps. There are maps from all over the world that draw for different purposes. During watch the history of the world map, you can feel the development of mapping technology. Also, you can experience the mapping technology of the future. MoMap is easily understood by anyone, not just by people who are interested in maps. The Experience Hall offers various programs to explore and experience map measurements and geographical information.

Target Audience

1. Persons and experts interested in rare maps collected from around the world.

2. Parents and children who want to study the history of map.

3. Persons who want to see another world through a map.

4. Persons who want to know how to see the world in the past.

5. Persons who curious about the development of modern mapping and future technology.

Logo Sketches

Logo Mark

To express the museum of the map, the logo has the characteristics of the map. The logo mark looks like a road and a building from above. The building is shaped like a letter, and geometric lines represent roads.
