MoMap

Branding Design

Designed a branding system for MoMap, a map museum, using geometric elements found on maps and colors found on Earth. Conducted research on the museum's history, mission, and target audience to create a logo and brand style guide for consistency. The result was a visually appealing and cohesive brand that helped the museum connect with its audience and stand out in the market.
