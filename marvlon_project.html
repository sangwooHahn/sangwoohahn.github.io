Marvlon is a strategy collection game designed to be optimized for mobile devices. The game is designed in portrait orientation for easy one-handed operation without the need to rotate the phone, and the goal is to complete various missions by combining simple strategies and various cards to grow your mercenary group and obtain rewards.
