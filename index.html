Hello!

I am Sangwoo Hahn! I’m a graphic designer from South Korea. I design in an interdisciplinary manner, but my goal is to make design more visually and systematically comfortable. I aim to sustain a visual lifestyle, through conducting social research and finding functional solutions to the problems of the present. Currently, I am most interested in UI / UX Design. I hope my abilities can offer better living through design for everyone.
