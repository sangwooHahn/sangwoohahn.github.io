Custom Websites for K12 Schools

Design System

A customizable webpage template was redesigned for over 60 schools, allowing each institution to tailor colors, fonts, and content through an intuitive admin interface. The project included migrating the site to WordPress, improving loading speed, and optimizing SEO. Collaboration with developers led to a complete restructuring of the design system, ensuring consistency across all components and a fully responsive experience across devices.

Role

UX/UI Designer, Design System Manager

Duration

May 2024 – Present

Goal

Over 60 customizable website templates for schools, tailored to meet each school’s needs.

Target Audience

School administrators and staff seeking an easy-to-use, customizable website platform.
