Game Design / UI · UX Design

The Fins

The Fins project is cooperation project with a programmer. The Fins is a tactical game that advocates an auto-defense game genre. The player can summon units to prevent the island and destroy enemy bases. The units automatically finds and attacks the nearest enemy.

Target Users

The Fins used simple and cute graphics and easy manipulation. It is a game designed so that even young women and children who like cute graphics can easily play and feel a sense of accomplishment. The automatic battle method has been chosen for ease of operation with a small screen smartphone. The game is designed in stage mode and takes 5 to 10 minutes per stage. Players can enjoy games even for a short time in their busy lives.

Character Sketches

The sea creature design considers of species diversity. The friendly units design with blue and gray-colored fish, but the enemy units are mollusks and deep-sea creatures. To show a detailed figure of the fish, half of their bodies are above water. The weapons and equipment that fish has are ocean waste from the human.
