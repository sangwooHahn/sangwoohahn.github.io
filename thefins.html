Game Design / UI · UX Design

The Fins

The Fins is a game development project in collaboration with a programmer. The Fins is a tactical game of the auto-defensive game genre. The player can summon the units to defend the island and destroy enemy bases. The units automatically find and attacks the nearest enemy.

Target Audience

The Fins is a simple game designed to make it easy for beginners to play. The game design with an automatic combat method to make it easier to operate with a small-screen smartphone. The game is designed in stage mode and takes 5 to 10 minutes per stage. Users can enjoy games in a short time in their busy lives.

Character Design

The fishes are chosen as characters to express the battle of a large scale. Fish divide into blue-gray and red colors according to friendly units or enemies. To show the details of the fishes, half of their bodies are out of the water.

Game Interface

UX Research

The process of the game is divide into preparation, combat, and results. The Players can upgrade units or island during the preparation phase and check their achievements. And then return to the game stage selection and start the battle. After the battle, the results and rewards can be obtained and used again in the preparation phase.

Resolution

Users summon characters where they want to and watch them fight. Fight enemies, earn rewards, get ready again, and challenge the next stage. The characters get stronger until they reach the final stage. It can get the same experience on both computers and PCs.
