Yes, I am a Graphic Designer!

Hello, I am Sangwoo Hahn, a graphic designer from Korea. I design in a variety of fields, but my goal is to make design more visually and systematically comfortable. I create visual lifestyle outcomes through the social research to find a functional solution to the problems of the past. I wish people live more comforting lives through my abilities.
