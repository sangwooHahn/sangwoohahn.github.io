MICARD
MICARD is a mobile card and account service for MICA students.

> Try Light mode
> Try Dark mode




Product Concept
MICARD is a mobile unit application service for students with NFC technology, mobile cards can now replace plastic cards. It has all the features of a student ID card, but is easier to use. For example, “Flex, Dining dollars, Meal plan” will be more effective and simpler to manage. Users can easily compare the menu of dining rooms and cafeterias from their MICARD and preorder and receive the notifications when the food is ready. In addition, Users can find out the status of the laundry in the dormitory, and the shuttle service for safe return at night.




Current Problems
MICA students have to carry their school ID card with them everywhere. Building to building, when they want to eat, or buy something. But all this behavior can be replaced by a simple app on your smartphone. Another problem is that, the original applications were not made at MICA. So students have to download different apps for each function. There may be more than five types of applications for a better school life, but some of them are old and do not update anymore.

Inspiration
Mobile ID cards take their inspiration from mobile bus tickets. The screen, with its uniform pattern of color bands, helps avoid fraudulent methods, such as screenshots. The blinking screen prove that it is not a picture. School accounts are inspired by banking applications and Apple Pay. Now users can easily control the balance and pay with their smartphone.
