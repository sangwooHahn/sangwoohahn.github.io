Product Concept
"MICARD" is a combination of word MICA and Card. MICARD is a mobile unite application service for students. It has all the features can do with student ID card. This app allows you to use NFC technology instead of carrying an existing student ID card. Three services offered from schools, "Flex, Dining dollars, Meal plan," will be more effective and simpler to manage. User can easily compare the menu of dining rooms and cafeterias. And preorder the foods and receive the notifications. Users can also find out the status of the laundry in the dormitory, and the shuttle service for safe return at night.
