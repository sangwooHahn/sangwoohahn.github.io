Thesis / Interaction Design

Transfont

Transfont is a project that connects the person and font. The design of the font depends on the person's movement. Font designs using one's body becomes the most personal font.

"How font design can connect to an individual?"

People communicate digitally in the digital age. People make avatars to express individuality or use emoticons to express their emotions. ut it doesn't show all the different characteristics that humans have. People have unique voices, facial expressions, gestures, gait, and handwriting. However, the letters transmitted through the Internet all use the same font without any individuality. Why can't people have their own fonts, just as they have their own voices or handwriting? The biggest reason is that the font design takes a lot of time and effort, and people have to design all the letters in the same design. To reduce this process and connect the font directly to the individual, conduct an experiment that connects the font with one of the characteristics of the individual. Transfont's fonts design through the movements of individuals.

Inspiration

The project started with the question, “Why don’t people make individual fonts?” and “In which way the font can be more reflective to the individual?” To answer this question, the Transfont connects through the interaction between the font and the users.

UX Research

To deliver the connection between the font and the users, “Interaction” is set as the main keyword. The user’s behavior reflects in the font and appears in the appearance of the font. Through this process, users perceive the font that reflects their actions as their font. Transfont created on a web page to make it easier for users to access users. Users can make fonts through their webcam and microphone. An alternative to webcams and microphones is to provide control bars and the text fields. Users can experience real-time fonts design that respond to their behavior.

Resolution

Transfont is a web-based project that easily accessible through the Internet. Various elements transform into user-specific combinations, allow them to create their unique fonts. The font design by the user can be download as an OTF format. Users can install this file and use the font.
