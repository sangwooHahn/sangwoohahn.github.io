InstaMusic

InstaMusic is a music streaming service design for Instagram users. Instagram users no longer need other music streaming service while they are on Instagram. InstaMusic users can look at users’ favorite stars or influencers’ playlists. Also, available on a webpage without installation or update.

Persona Scenario

Fandom, influencer, and music lovers choose as personas. Each of the personas shows three main features of InstaMusic. The fandom finds playlists from favorite followers (artists), then likes and listens to the music. The influencer shares a new playlist to their fans. The music lover go to the music lists and saves the music that he wants to hear.

Inspiration

The advantage of InstaMusic is that using the Instagram’s share system. Instagram feed design shifts into a music player through research on many applications such as Apple Music, Spotify, and Melon Player. To maintain the image-oriented design concept of the existing Shop and IG TV, InstaMusic applies a large album image and line-style icon.

Usability Tests

A fan's point of view

· Can you find your following list?
· Can you find your favorite singer (B1A4) on the list?
· Do you know where to go to see B1A4's personal playlists?
· Do you know how to save a song from B1A4's playlists?
· Do you know where you can see all the saved songs?

Influencers' point of view

· Do you know where to go to start creating a playlist?
· Can you delete the song "Hold My Hand" from the list?
· Can you name your playlists?
· After you have created your playlist, can you share it?

No need multitask

· Can you play the song on your playlists from where you are?
· Can you tell the name of the song that you are listening to?
· While you are listening, can you see the list of the songs?
· Tell me the name of the next song.
· Can you find others' comments on your playlist feed?
