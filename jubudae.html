Ju Budae :
Digital Menu

The digital menu is a menu designed for Budae-Jjigae restaurants. It is place on each table to help costumers quickly order by viewing the images without calling the waiter.

Current Problems

Budae-jjigae is a food that contains various ingredients and can add several times. To make food orders easier, the menu design as a digital menu. Customers can choose and order their own without calling a waiter whenever they order additional toppings.

UX Research

The process of using the digital menu divides into 3 stages: first order, extra order, and after eating. At the order stage, customers order basic Budae-jjigae. And add the toppings they want. On extra orders, customers can order for more toppings. After the eating, customers can check the price and get the bill.

Questions

· Can you change it to a different kind of Budae?
· Do you understand why the price varies depending on the number of people?
· Can you select and cancel the desired topping?
· Can you order 3 bottles of Coke?
· can you notice the total amount and the amount including tax?
· Can you cancel one coke you selected?
· Can you go back to the previous step and place an additional order?
· What should you do to call a waiter?

Resolution

Budae-jjigae is a food that contains various ingredients and can add several times. To make food orders easier, the menu design as a digital menu. Digital menus have reduced the process of calling waiters, ordering extra food, and receiving food again. And customers can order unfamiliar food with seeing the photos.
